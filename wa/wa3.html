New Girl

Overview

New Girl is an American sitcom that originally aired from 2011 to 2018 on Fox. The series revolves around teacher Jessica Day as she moves into a new Los Angeles apartment with three men, Nick Miller, Schmidt, and Winston Bishop (though in the pilot ep Bishop was a character named Coach).

Plot Summary

After getting cheated on by her long term boyfriend, Jess Day quickly moves into a Los Angeles loft with three random men. Nick Miller, one of the roommates, is a bartender and aspiring writer who dropped out of law school. Another one of the roommates, Schmidt (name later revealed to be Winston Schidmt) works at a marketing firm and is uptight and considers himself to be a ladies man. The final roommate is Winston Bishop who is Nick's childhood friend that is just returning from playing professional basketball oversees. In the pilot the third roommate was a character called Coach, but he had to be replaced as there was contractual conflict with the actor playing Coach.
